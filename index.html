Privacy Policy

It Fits Tho  ·  Last updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how It Fits Tho ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or location).

However, the App uses third-party services that may collect certain data automatically:

  • Device information (device model, OS version)
  • Advertising ID (used to serve relevant ads)
  • IP address (used for approximate location for ad targeting)
  • App interaction data (ad impressions, clicks)

2. Advertising – Google AdMob

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy to provide personalised or non-personalised ads.

  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses data from apps: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You can opt out of personalised advertising on your device:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

3. Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only using shared preferences:

  • Your best score
  • Your selected theme (dark / light)
  • Your selected language

This data never leaves your device and is not shared with us or any third party.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

5. Permissions

The App requests the following device permissions:

  • INTERNET – required to load and display advertisements.
  • HIGH_SAMPLING_RATE_SENSORS – used solely to detect phone shake gestures for the in-game piece reshuffle feature. No sensor data is stored or transmitted.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
